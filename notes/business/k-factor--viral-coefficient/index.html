K-Factor (Viral Coefficient)

2025-01-31 :: 1 min read (33 words)

What is it?⌗

The K-factor is the average number of referral signups, per unique inviting user.

K-Factor Scale⌗

  • 0.15 - 0.20 = ideal minimum
  • 0.40 = good
  • 0.70 = outstanding

  • 1 = viral
