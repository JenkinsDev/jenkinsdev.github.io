BBQ Sauce Ratios

2024-02-02 :: 1 min read (37 words)

“Red” Tomato-based BBQ Sauce⌗

Per 4 servings:

  • 1/2 cup Tomato Sauce
  • ~1 Tbsp of acidity (e.g. vinegars)
  • ~2 Tbsp of sweet (e.g. brown sugar, molasses)
  • ~1-3 Tbsp of spicy (e.g. hot sauce)
  • Seasoning (warm, earthy + salt)
