Key Words of Astronomy

2025-06-05 :: 1 min read (58 words)

Astronomy: A branching field of Science that focuses on the study of Planetary Science, Stellar Astronomy, Galactic Astronomy and more!

Astrophysics: A branch of astronomy thay focuses on the study of celestial objects and phenomena (e.g. stars, planets, asteroids, black holes, etc.). Primarily focused on unearthing the physical processes and rules that govern the behavior of these objects.
